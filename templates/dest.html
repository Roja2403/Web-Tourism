DESTINATIONS IN OOTY

Avalanche Lake

Located 28 km away from Ooty, Avalanche lake is a must visit when on Ooty Tour. This lake, beautifully lying amid the lush greenery of mountains and landscapes mesmerises every visitor. Popular here are camping, rafting and trekking to nearby hilly regions such as Upper Bhavani. It is also an ideal picnic spot.

Address 28 kms from Ooty, The Nilgiris, Ooty, Tamil Nadu, 643001

Timing Mon to sun(9a.m to 3p.m)

Ooty Botanical Gardens

Maintained by the Horticultural Department of Tamil Nadu, Botanical Gardens of Ooty is undeniably one of the most popular places to see in Ooty. This garden is divided into five different sections such as Fern House, Lower Garden, Italian Garden, Conservatory and Nurseries.

Address Nilgiris,Vannarapettai,Ooty,Tamil Nadu,643001

Timing Mon to sun(7a.m to 6.30pm)

Mudumalai National Park

Connecting Eastern Ghats and the Western Ghats, on the Ooty Mysore route, Mudumalai National Park is another place to visit by Nature lovers when on their tour to Ooty. This national park boasts of many wild animals and rich avian fauna along with equally rich flora. Tiger reserve as it is home to approximately 50 tigers

Address NH 67, Gudalur NH 67, Gudalur, Devarshola, Tamil Nadu, 643223

Timing Mon to sun(6a.m to 6p.m)
